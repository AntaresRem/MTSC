This Isn't Your Typical Gym

MTSC started in the local parks of East London. We didn't have mirrors, fancy lighting, or ego. We just had pads, grass, and a shared drive to get better.

Today, we've moved indoors, but those park roots stay with us. It's about more than just a workout—it's about finding your people in the city and building something real.
