Tailored Learning Streams

We recognize that a 5-year-old and a 12-year-old have different developmental needs. Our classes are strictly divided by age to ensure appropriate coaching.

Ages 5 - 8

Little Lions

Focus on listening skills, basic motor control, and introduction to the '8 Limbs' through fun, engaging drills.

  • Fundamental Balance
  • Discipline & Etiquette
  • Movement Awareness
Ages 9 - 12

Junior Warriors

Transitioning towards technical proficiency, strategy, and partner work in a controlled, respectful environment.

  • Advanced Striking Tech
  • Strategy & Focus
  • Mentorship Opportunities
