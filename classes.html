FAQs

What should I bring? expand_more

For your first session:

  • Workout clothes (shorts and t-shirt)
  • Water bottle
  • Towel
  • Hand wraps (available for purchase at the gym if needed)
Can I join as a complete beginner? expand_more

Yes. We have dedicated Foundation classes designed for those with no prior experience. Our coaches will guide you through the basics at your own pace.

Where are the gyms located? expand_more

We have two studios: Shoreditch (near Old Street Station) and Hackney (near London Fields). Both are fully equipped with bags and rings.
